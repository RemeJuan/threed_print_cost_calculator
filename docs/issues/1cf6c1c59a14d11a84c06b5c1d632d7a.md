## Summary
Fatal Android crash in G-code import flow. Crash occurs while Flutter's `StandardMessageCodec` encodes a very large payload returned through `file_selector_android`, causing `ByteArrayOutputStream` growth to exceed available heap.

## Suspected cause
Large file-selection result or related payload encoded over Flutter platform channel during Android G-code import. Crash is in third-party/plugin boundary (`file_selector_android` + `StandardMessageCodec`), not app business logic directly.
