3D Print Cost Calculator

Calculate the true cost of your 3D prints including filament, electricity, failure risk, labour, and overhead in one fast local-first workflow.

The Problem

Filament-only pricing misses the actual print cost. Real-world quoting includes multiple cost drivers:

Filament Alone Is Incomplete

Many makers only price by grams used, which underestimates your final cost.

Electricity Is Printer-Specific

Power draw varies by machine and print length, and should be part of every quote.

Failed Prints Are Real Loss

A realistic failure risk percentage changes your expected cost outcome.

Labour and Overhead Matter

Prep, post-processing, and running costs are often ignored but directly impact margin.

The Solution

The app calculates true end-to-end print cost with a detailed, understandable breakdown.

  • Filament cost calculation with multi-material support
  • Electricity cost integration per printer profile
  • Failure risk modelling
  • Labour and processing time input
  • Detailed line-item cost breakdown
