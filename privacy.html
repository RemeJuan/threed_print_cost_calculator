Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Overview

3D Print Cost Calculator is designed with a local-first approach. Core cost calculations are performed on-device.

Data Collection

  • No account is required to use the app.
  • Print calculation data is stored locally on your device.
  • The app does not require cloud sync for core features.

Analytics

The app may collect limited product analytics events to understand feature usage and improve reliability. No print names, job payloads, or user identifiers are intentionally sent as part of core analytics.

Website Data

This GitHub Pages site is static and does not provide account login, user uploads, or server-side data storage.

Third-Party Stores

Download links direct to Apple App Store and Google Play. Those platforms process data under their own privacy policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact google@remej.dev.
